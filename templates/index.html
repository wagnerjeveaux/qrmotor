Welcome to the Vehicle Management System

This system allows users to manage their vehicles efficiently, keeping track of their details and maintenance history. Each vehicle registered in the system will have a unique QR Code generated, making it easy to access the vehicle's history.

How to Use:

  • Register a Vehicle: Click the "Add Car" button to add a new vehicle to your account. Provide the vehicle's license plate, model, and year.
  • View Vehicle History: For each vehicle, click the "Add History" button to add maintenance records or view existing ones.
  • Access QR Code: Click the "QR Code" button next to a vehicle to view or scan its unique QR Code. This code links directly to the vehicle's maintenance history.

Features:

  • Secure login to ensure your data remains private.
  • Each user can only see and manage their own vehicles.
  • Generate and access unique QR Codes for easy sharing of vehicle history.
  • Simple and user-friendly interface.

Get Started:

Use the navigation bar to explore the system. Start by registering a vehicle or viewing your existing cars. Enjoy a seamless experience in managing your vehicle data!
